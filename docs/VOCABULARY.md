# Freelance-OS Ubiquitous Language

This document defines the core terminology and concepts used within the Freelance-OS project. Adhering to this vocabulary ensures consistency and clarity in all communications and code.

**Key Concepts:**

*   **Lead:** A potential client or opportunity identified.
*   **Proposal:** A detailed document outlining services, scope, and pricing for a specific Lead.
*   **Client:** A Lead that has accepted a Proposal and is actively engaged for services.
*   **Project:** A defined piece of work undertaken for a Client.
*   **Invoice:** A financial document requesting payment for services rendered.
*   **Retainer:** An ongoing agreement with a Client for continuous services.
*   **Contract:** A legally binding agreement outlining terms between Freelance-OS and a Client.
*   **Task:** A specific action item within a Project.
*   **Milestone:** A significant achievement or deliverable within a Project.
*   **Skill:** A professional capability offered by Freelance-OS.

**Acronyms & Abbreviations:**

*   ADR: Architectural Decision Record
*   HLD: High-Level Design
*   LLD: Low-Level Design

**Usage Notes:**

*   Always use the defined terms precisely.
*   When introducing new concepts, propose them for inclusion in this vocabulary.
